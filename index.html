My work sits at the intersection of analytics, marketing, and business strategy, and I enjoy using data to help organizations solve complex problems and make more informed, robust decisions.

Before graduate school, I worked at Sky Services where I analyzed acquisition and customer lifecycle data to help leadership better understand sales pipeline performance, identify growth opportunities, and improve revenue forecasting. I bring a blend of analytical rigor, business perspective, and collaborative mindset that allows me to contribute meaningfully in environments where teams are solving complex problems with data.
